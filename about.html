Global Link travels

GLOBAL LINK TRAVELS ltd is the leading travel agency in Rwanda started in 2017 with well trained and experienced staff to serve your travel needs 7 days a week.

We offer air tickets for international and domestic, hotel bookings, car rentals, treatment abroad facility and tour packages etc.

Global link travel ltd is licensed travel agency by Rwanda development board and member of Rwanda association of travel agencies .We establish our business and will continue to serve with excellent customer care services .we are committed to help our customer “most say we are the best in the travel business”.

At Global link travels we take pride to serve your travel needs and we look forward for the opportunity to help you plan and book your next vacation.
